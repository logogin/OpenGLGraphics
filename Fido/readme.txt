Fido-fast attacking cobot v2.204alpha
-------------------------------------
If you have problems with link, enter to Project/Settings/Link and add to 
Object/library modules - glaux.lib

Control buttons:
----------------
p-switch between perspective and ortho
s-move light left
d-move light right
e-move light up
c-move light down
f-move light forward
b-move light backward
i,j,k-change spectr of light
a-switch to fullscreen
m-stop/start motion
z-return window to original size and position
<,>-resize window
esc-quit
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Moving parts of Fido:
---------------------
Pressing right mouse button will pop up a menu, after choosing a part of an object
press left arrow or right arrow to move this part.

Moving around the scene:
------------------------
By pressing left mouse button and holding it, you can move around the scene.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
For best view change resolution of your screen to 1024x768.
